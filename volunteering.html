My Volunteering Experience

Here, I reveal all the places I have volunteered to make a difference!

Why do I volunteer?

There are many reasons I volunteer: making a difference in the world around me, getting involved with like-minded individuals in my community, and gain valuable experiences that will assist me in future. I specifically like volunteering in STEAM-related positions where I am most passionate about the work. I also really enjoy teaching or tutoring positions where I can directly influence someone else's life positively by assisting them to reach their most desired goals!

Youth Empowering Parents

Volunteering at Youth Empowering Parents was a wonderful experience in the summer of 2022. Using my technological knowledge in various programs and softwares, I successfully tutored many elderly people and my own mother. This opportunity gave me knowledge into making learning plans efficiently, finding the environment a person learns best in, and finally how intimidating new concepts can be to beginners.

Musical Concerts

I have particpated in 3 musical concerts for my school and many more musical competitions. These events have been well perfected and rehearsed for over 30 hours each, with rehearsals lasting 5 hours after school at times. I find these experiences highly valuable as I have shown myself how committed I become when doing something I am passionate about. The most important skill I learned in these concerts and competitions was synchrony: I learned to effectively work together with over 200 other musicians to deliver beauitful pieces of music to the crowds.

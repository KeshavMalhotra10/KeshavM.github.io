About me

A little about myself and how I choose to spend my time!

Who am I?

I am a determined student working to solve issues and problems in my community. I love helping other people with my work, which is why I am part of several groups that aim to better the community. I openly challenge myself to always be learning something as I feel the more I learn, the more I can benefit the world around me. See what I value most below!

Nature

I love spending time in nature, it allows me to relax and think about what is important to me. I feel like my best ideas have always sprouted from thinking in silence in nature. I often also go out for walks or bike rides with my friends in nature and it is one part of my life that has remained constant when many things have changed.

Family

I value my family over all else as they have supported me through my victories and comforted me through my defeats. My family has played a vital role in my growth as a person; the experiences we have shared together have taught me countless skills such as self-reliance, perserverance, and the value of our possessions that we take for granted. I always prioritize my family first and wish to take care of them as I continuously work to help other's lives around me.

Education

Education has always been important to me. I was always privileged enough to have my school life taken care for me. This feels extremely valuable for me as my parents growing up were not so fortunate to easily afford their education without working on the side to support their education and their families. Everyone close to me supports me in my education and this is why I make sure I always prioritze it. This pursuit of education has given me the opportunity to be acknowledged with several academic awards and certificates.

Music
